Name the Kraken!

The kraken is a legendary creature from Scandinavian folklore, said to dwell off the coast of Norway and Greenland. With its enormous size and tentacles, it is known to terrorize sailors and ships by dragging them into the depths of the ocean. Descriptions often paint the kraken as a massive, octopus-like creature, capable of creating whirlpools and wreaking havoc on any ship that crosses its path.
